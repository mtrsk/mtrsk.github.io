#+title: Erlang Debugger
#+HUGO_CATEGORIES: "Functional Programming"

To start the debugger, you can call ~debugger:start()~ from the [[id:bcd04f60-0a4c-4213-90f7-139c0ddf5bef][Erlang Shell]]:

#+begin_src shell
  1> debugger:start().
#+end_src
